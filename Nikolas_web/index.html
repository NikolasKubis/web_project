Welcome





My research is in the areas of stochastic control and deterministic filtering on matrix Lie groups. I am also interested in reinforcement learning and in risk-averse decision making.


I was a postgraduate researcher with the Department of Attitude and Orbit Control System at Airbus Defence and Space (Friedrichshafen). Prior to that, I received my MSc from the 3ME's Delft Center of Systems and Control (DCSC) at TU Delft where I worked with Prof. Bart De Schutter. I graduated from the Electrical & Computer Engineering at the University of Patras being advised by Prof. George Bitsoris and Prof. Thanos Stouraitis.
